Overview

A Probabilistic Reasoning As Symbolic Evaluation (PRAiSE) library, developed at SRI International's Artificial Intelligence Centern, which provides capabilities in the following areas:

  • Lifted First-Order Probabilistic Inference.
  • Support for defining First-Order Probabilistic Models.
  • Support for performing inference on (fragments of) Church probabilistic programs.
